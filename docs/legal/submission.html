Submission & Verification Policy

We review business submissions to confirm they represent Native-owned businesses and meet our standards.

Requirements

  • Owner identifies as Native American, Alaska Native, or Indigenous.
  • Information provided is accurate, current, and truthful.
  • Submitter is authorized to represent the business or has the business’s permission.

Verification Approach

  • We review submissions for completeness and clarity.
  • We do not independently verify Tribal enrollment or citizenship.
  • We may contact the submitter for clarification; incomplete or conflicting details may be declined.

Review Time

Most submissions are reviewed within 24–72 hours, depending on volume.

Corrections and Removals

  • Owners may request edits or removal of their listing.
  • Listings may be paused or removed if they violate our policies or appear inaccurate.
