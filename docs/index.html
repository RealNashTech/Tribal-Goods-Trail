About Tribal Goods Trail

Tribal Goods Trail was created to support and uplift Native-owned businesses, artisans, vendors, and community sellers across Oregon, the Pacific Northwest, and—eventually—the entire United States. Whether someone operates a licensed storefront or sells beadwork, foods, crafts, or cultural items independently, they deserve visibility and support.

This app is inspired by my longtime connection to the Confederated Tribes of Warm Springs. My wife and children come from Warm Springs, and I spent 15 years living and working in the community. Tribal Goods Trail is one way I honor that connection while giving back in a meaningful way.

My path hasn’t been perfect, but building this app is part of my commitment to growth, accountability, and supporting Native entrepreneurs with respect and accuracy.

I do not claim Tribal affiliation. This platform simply highlights Native-owned sellers who choose to share their work, skills, and culture with others.

Tribal Goods Trail listings are always free for Native-owned businesses and community sellers. No account is required to use the app, and the map updates live whenever a submission is approved.
